SERVICES

    Link Up - Mentoring

  • Link Up is a powerful support to encourage people manage their own learning and experiences in order to maximize their potential and develop skills in order to become the person they want to be.

    Our mentoring service is an effective way of helping people to progress in their daily life and is a personal development and empowerment tool.

    It is a partnership between two people ( the mentor and the mentee) who are normally studying in the same field of maybe sharing similar experiences.

  • The mentor will be the person guiding the mentee and helping him/ her find the right direction and help develop social and academic skills.Mentors rely upon having had the same or similar experiences before in order to gain empathy with the mentee.

    The mentor will always try to help the mentee in providing encouragement and guidance,in becoming more self aware and taking responsibilities in life.

    Our mentoring service is to help you believe in yourself and boost your self confidence, it is an opportunity to push your life in the right direction.
