WELCOME TO SALAM ASSOCIATION

Our mission to represent the Arabian Culture is eternal. It revolves over the idea of creating leaders in our community through knowledge and integration. Here at Salam, we strive to unite and empower students and people with the ability to grow and learn from one another for the greater cause of creating a stronger Arab community.

Explore what Salam is, who we are, and what we strive to do through our works and events and feel free to contact us should you have any questions or would like to contribute to its success.

  • Salam Games

    2 Dec 2013

    Salam Games, an event that unites the Arab community through fun, entertaining and educative gaming while influencing other communities in regards to the kind of games Arabs enjoy. From the traditional Nacka gammon and card games, such as Trix and Tarneeb, to the modern competitive games of Ping Pong and FIFA. The unity of all is what brings the happiness amongst us all. Stay tuned for our next Salam Games!
