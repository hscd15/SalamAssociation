HISTORY

The Founder

The Salam Association, previously known as Salam Group, was founded by Ahmad Kasim Rashid, during his university year of 2008. The idea of starting this organization came about from the lack of unity between the Arabic communities. He built Salam based on the representation of the Arabian culture, and sooner than later, it pushed the differences between the Arabic communities aside and brought them together, impacting the image of the Arabian culture. That impact gave the opportunity for everyone to be properly introduced to the Arabian culture.
