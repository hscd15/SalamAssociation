UPCOMING EVENTS

2013

Salam Games

Monday, December 2, 2013 at 9:00am

Location

Atrium, University Centre, Carleton University

Salam Games, an event that unites the Arab community through fun, entertaining and educative gaming while influencing other communities in regards to the kind of games Arabs enjoy. From the traditional Nacka gammon and card games, such as Trix and Tarneeb, to the modern competitive games of Ping Pong and FIFA. The unity of all is what brings the happiness amongst us all. Stay tuned for our next Salam Games!
